At Heart Interior Company is a leading Company in Upholstery and Furniture Repair in East Africa. It mainly deals in the Make and Repair of furniture with soft,padded textile covering features, such as Armchairs, Sofas, Couch, beds etc... At Heart Interior Company was formed in 2020 and launched its operations in the same year. Its a Partnership Company with only two Board Members and looks forward at distributing its shares to potential investors.
